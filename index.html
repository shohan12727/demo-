The vision of Bangladesh

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and rebust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investment in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
